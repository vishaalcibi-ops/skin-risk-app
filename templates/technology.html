Our Technology

Exploring the AI architecture behind DermaAI's advanced analysis.

MobileNetV2 Architecture

DermaAI is powered by MobileNetV2, a revolutionary light-weight convolutional neural network. It is specifically designed for mobile and embedded vision applications, making it incredibly fast without sacrificing identification accuracy.

Transfer Learning

We leverage Transfer Learning, initializing our model with weights from ImageNet. This allows the AI to recognize complex patterns and textures in skin images with significantly less training data than traditional methods.

Efficient Inversion

MobileNetV2 uses "inverted residuals" and "linear bottlenecks," which allow the model to process high-dimensional features while remaining computationally efficient on server-side and mobile environments.

Feature Extraction

The model analyzes spatial hierarchies, identifying everything from macroscopic lesion shapes to microscopic texture irregularities and color distribution patterns.

Deterministic Logic

Our analysis pipeline includes a deterministic verification layer that ensures consistency and variety, providing bespoke feedback for every unique image uploaded.
