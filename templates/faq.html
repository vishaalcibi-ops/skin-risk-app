Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about DermaAI and skin health.

How accurate is the AI identification?

DermaAI uses MobileNetV2, a state-of-the-art neural network trained on thousands of clinical images. While highly accurate in detecting common traits, it is intended as a screening tool and should never replace a professional medical diagnosis.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your images are processed securely and stored only in your private scan history. We use industry-standard encryption and do not share your medical data with third parties.

What should I do if the AI identifies a high-risk condition?

If the AI flags a "High Risk" condition (like Melanoma), you should consult a certified dermatologist immediately. The AI provides a "Doctor Advice" section with specific questions you should ask during your appointment.

Can I scan any part of my body?

The AI is trained for common skin surfaces. For best results, ensure the image is clear, well-lit, and focused directly on the area of concern. Avoid blurry or extremely close-up "macro" shots without context.
